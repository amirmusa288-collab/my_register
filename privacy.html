Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MyRegister.online ("we", "us", "the Site") handles information when you use My Register. By using this Site, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Your Register Content Stays on Your Device

My Register does not require an account. Everything you write — whether typed text, Urdu text, hand-drawn notes, page labels, or bookmarks — is stored directly in your browser's local storage, on your own device. None of the content you write on any page is uploaded to our servers, read by us, or shared with anyone. If you clear your browser data or switch devices, your register will not automatically follow you, since nothing is backed up remotely.

2. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use browser storage for one purpose: remembering your pages, labels, bookmarks, and preferences between visits on the same device. We also use Google AdSense to display ads on this Site. Google and its advertising partners may use cookies (including the DoubleClick DART cookie) to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Blocking cookies may affect how the Site remembers your register content.

3. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

This Site uses Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this Site based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies by visiting www.aboutads.info.

4. Analytics

We may use analytics tools to understand aggregate, anonymized traffic patterns, such as how many people visit the Site. This data does not include the content of your register and does not identify you personally.

5. Children's Privacy

My Register is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

Since your register content is stored only in your own browser, you are always in control of it — you can delete any page's content at any time from within the app, or clear your browser's site data to remove everything at once. For questions about data collected through advertising cookies, see Section 3 above, or contact us on our Contact page.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the Site itself, but since your register content lives on your own device, its security also depends on your device and browser being kept secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent via our Contact page.
