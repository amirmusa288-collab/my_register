About My Register

Last updated: July 2026

My Register was built around a simple observation: most note-taking apps are more complicated than the thing they're replacing. Before smartphones, people kept a physical register — a numbered notebook where you opened it, wrote something down, and remembered the page number. It worked because it was simple. My Register brings that same idea online, without turning it into another app full of folders, tags, and sign-up screens.

What Makes It Different

My Register has exactly 100 numbered pages, just like a real notebook. You can write on any page using your keyboard — in English or Urdu — or write and draw by hand using your finger or a mouse, the same way you would with a pen. Every page turn plays a small animation and a soft sound, because a register should feel like a register, not a plain text box.

Who We Are

We're a small independent team building simple, honest tools that don't ask more of you than they need to. My Register is one of our first projects.
